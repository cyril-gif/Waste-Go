FAQ

How do I request a pickup?

Tap "Request Pickup" on the Home screen, choose your load size and payment method, then confirm. A rider will be matched within minutes.

How do riders earn?

Riders earn 75% of every job. For cash payments, the commission is automatically deducted from the rider's WasteGo wallet. Earnings are tracked in real time.

Is cash payment safe?

Yes. When you pay cash, the platform commission is auto-deducted from the rider's wallet balance. The rider must have sufficient wallet balance to accept cash jobs.
