FAQ

How do I request a pickup?

Tap "Request Pickup" on the Home screen, choose your load size, then confirm. A rider will be matched to you within minutes.

How does MoMo payment work?

After your waste is collected, you pay via MTN MoMo, Vodafone Cash, or AirtelTigo. We use Hubtel's secure API — your number is never stored.

What if the fare is wrong?

Riders can submit a photo to request a fare adjustment. You must approve any change before payment is processed. You can also contact support.

How do riders earn?

Riders earn 75% of every completed job. The platform takes 25%. Earnings are tracked in real time on the Rider Dashboard.
