How WasteGo Works

From request to collection in minutes — powered by smart matching and Ghana's mobile money ecosystem.

01
📍

Request a Pickup

Open WasteGo, choose your waste load size (Small / Medium / Large / Bulk), and see an instant price estimate before you confirm.

02
🛺

Rider Gets Matched

The nearest available tricycle rider is automatically matched to your request. Track their location in real time as they head to you.

03
📱

Pay with MoMo

Once collection is complete, pay instantly via MTN MoMo, Vodafone Cash, or AirtelTigo. No cash, no friction.

Platform Features

Hybrid Pricing Engine
